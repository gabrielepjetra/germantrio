About the Creator

Hi, I'm Gabriele.

As an Audi owner and a lifelong car enthusiast, I know that choosing between the "Big Three" can be a struggle.

Online forums often turn simple questions into an all-out war. Instead of getting clear answers, you end up with more doubts than when you started, depending on which brand the community favors more.

That is why I created The German Trio (TGT). My goal is to stop the noise and provide a neutral platform where you can compare models based on your actual needs—whether you're looking for pure sportiness, ultimate comfort, or the perfect mix of both.

"I built this for you to find the car that truly fits your life, without the bias of online debates."
