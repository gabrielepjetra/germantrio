German Automotive Brands

Audi

Founded in 1909 by August Horch, Audi has always focused on technological innovation and precise engineering. Its famous motto “Vorsprung durch Technik” reflects the brand's philosophy of always being a step ahead in technology. In the 1980s, Audi revolutionized the market with its Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Today, it is known for elegant sedans, high-performance sports cars, and digitally advanced interiors. Audi has also heavily invested in electrification, with models like the e-tron and the RS e-tron GT. The company represents a combination of luxury, sportiness, and German innovation.

Flagship Model: Audi RS 6 Avant Performance

BMW

Founded in 1916 as an aircraft engine manufacturer, BMW later specialized in motorcycles and luxury cars. The brand is famous for its philosophy of “Sheer Driving Pleasure,” emphasizing driver enjoyment and dynamic performance. In the 1970s, it introduced iconic models like the 3 Series, still a benchmark for sporty sedans today. BMW is synonymous with high performance and recognizable design, especially the aggressive lines of the M Series. Technology and innovation have always been at the core, with advanced driver assistance systems and efficient engines. Today, BMW combines luxury, sportiness, and comfort while maintaining the strong identity of German automotive tradition.

Flagship Model: BMW M5 Competition

Mercedes-Benz

Founded in 1926 from the merger of Benz & Cie and Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft, Mercedes-Benz is a symbol of luxury and innovation. It is considered a pioneer in the automotive industry, introducing many safety and comfort systems for the first time. The range includes elegant sedans and powerful AMG models, always guaranteeing quality and refinement. Mercedes has made history with iconic models like the S-Class and G-Class, true automotive legends. The brand focuses on balancing performance, advanced technology, and uncompromising comfort. Mercedes-Benz remains a global reference for those seeking luxury, safety, and timeless design.

Flagship Model: Mercedes-AMG GT 63 S 4MATIC+
